Antenna Mini - User Instructions

Setup & Configuration:
1. Open Antenna Mini from your Desktop.
2. The UI provides quick switching for your N1MM radio and ESP network relays.
3. Click the ⚙ (Gear) icon to open Settings, then click "EDIT XML CONFIG".
4. Notepad will open. Edit your IP address, ESP identifiers, and custom frequencies.
5. Save the file and close Notepad. The application will instantly auto-reboot to apply your changes!

Using N1MM Auto-Switch:
- Check the "N1MM" box to listen to N1MM Logger+ frequency broadcasts.
- If N1MM goes offline or is closed, the app will safely revert to Manual Control after 20 seconds of silence to ensure you never lose a connection during a dropout.
- You can manually override at any time by unchecking the box.
